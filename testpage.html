Daily Rhythm

A gentle routine you can keep even on “meh” days. Circle what you actually do, not what you wish you did.

Household: ____________
Pets: ☐ Yes ☐ No

Morning Reset (5–15 min)

  • Unload dishwasher / clear drying rack
  • Trash scan (kitchen + bathroom)
  • Wipe counters (kitchen “stripe”)
  • Start one laundry load (if needed)
  • Make bed OR tidy bedroom pathway

Evening Close (10–20 min)

  • Kitchen close: dishes + counters + sink
  • Put away “floater items” (5-minute sweep)
  • Set tomorrow’s top 3 chores
  • Quick bathroom wipe if needed
  • Start dishwasher / take out trash if full

Kitchen Standards

Pick your “clean enough” definition.

  • Sink empty at end of day
  • Counters cleared in one main zone
  • Trash not overflowing
  • Fridge: obvious spoilage removed

Laundry Flow

Use steps so laundry doesn’t become a mountain.

  • Sort basket(s): Lights / Darks / Towels
  • One load per day OR two loads twice/week
  • Fold for 10 minutes (timer) — then stop
  • Put away “hot items” first (socks/underwear)
