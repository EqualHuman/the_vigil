About LabContext

Modern lab education • labcontext.org

LabContext exists to make laboratory medicine easier to understand — without making it feel clinical. The goal is clarity: how tests relate to physiology, how oversight works, and how to think in patterns instead of lists.

What LabContext is

  • Education: structured explanations that don’t assume prior context.
  • Interactive: short modules students can finish and teachers can assign.
  • Blog: commentary and synthesis — clearly labeled as such.

What LabContext isn’t

  • Not a clinical simulator
  • Not medical advice
  • Not a substitute for training or policy
Design principle: the site should feel modern and engaging — not like another clinical handout.
