This page is the high-level outline for the template library. The aim is to keep every moving part in one glanceable place: page layouts, reusable content blocks, interaction systems, and quiet support templates that hold everything together.

When a template moves from idea to draft to live, only this page needs to change. Sets can be archived without losing their story, and new tracks can grow beneath this one for future sub-projects.

How to use for future you
