Weather

Forks is famously unpredictable. Light rain is always possible, and temperatures can drop quickly in the evening.

We will proceed outdoors. If conditions require, we’ll make small adjustments for comfort and safety.

If you’re traveling from out of town, pack layers and a rain-resistant outer layer.

Photography

We ask for an unplugged ceremony—please keep phones silent and put away until after we’ve walked back down the aisle.

After the ceremony, we’ll have time for candid photos together.

Music

The reception will include light music, dinner, and dancing. If you have a song you’d love to hear, add it in your RSVP note.

Getting there

Forks is on Washington’s Olympic Peninsula. Most out-of-town guests fly into Seattle–Tacoma International Airport (SEA) and drive to Forks.

  • Plan for a longer drive than expected—roads are scenic and can be slow.
  • Cell service may be limited near the ceremony location.

Lodging

A limited number of rooms have been reserved in Forks and the surrounding area. Additional options are available in Port Angeles and nearby coastal towns.

Lodging details and reservation information will be shared with confirmed guests.

Parking at the ceremony location is limited. Transportation instructions will be provided to confirmed guests.
