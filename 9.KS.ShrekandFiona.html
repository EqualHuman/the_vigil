The Swamp Guide

Getting There

The Swamp Estate is intentionally “tucked away.” Follow the lantern markers and keep to the boardwalk. If you arrive at a perfectly manicured castle, you’ve gone the wrong way.

Parking

Complimentary parking is available in the “Dry Patch” area. From there, a short lantern-lit path leads to the ceremony. Shuttle carts will run as needed.

Helpful Tips

  • Boardwalks may be dewy — choose stable shoes.
  • Bring a light wrap; swamp evenings can be cool.
  • If you see a sign that says “KEEP OUT,” it’s decorative.

Navigation Phrase

If someone asks who you’re here for: “The ogre with the big heart.”

Swamp Etiquette (Yes, It’s a Thing)

  • Please do not “tidy up” the moss arrangements — they are intentionally wild.
  • Compliments to the couple are welcome; unsolicited “princess” opinions are not.
  • Respect the resident wildlife. They live here. You’re visiting.
  • Keep laughter loud and judgments quiet.

This is an event where authenticity is the dress code underneath the dress code.

Photo Moments

You’re welcome to take photos throughout the evening. For the ceremony, we ask you keep phones low and hearts high.

“No Drama” Policy

If you feel the urge to stir trouble, please step outside and speak to the frogs. They’re excellent listeners.
