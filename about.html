About

vigil • vigilia • vigiliae [f.] — watch; vigil; wakefulness. Vigil is a quiet watchfulness over how humans treat one another and the living world. It collects stories, data, and resources that show where we have harmed — and where we try to repair.
About jobberish
We have struggled. We have harmed. But we have also healed, innovated, and learned. This Vigil is our shared observation — of what we have done, and what we may still undo.
How this space works
Vigil is...
  • a space for observing how our choices shape other lives
  • a collection of researched threads, not breaking news
  • a way to make complex issues quieter, slower, and more understandable
  • a reminder that harm and repair both belong in the story
Vigil is not...
  • a replacement for legal, medical, or financial advice
  • a platform for outrage for its own sake
  • a place where any one person is the hero, expert, or main character
This Vigil continues as long as we continue to watch.
